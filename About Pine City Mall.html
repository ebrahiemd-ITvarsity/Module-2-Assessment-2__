Pine City Mall is the ultimate fashion, entertainment and dining destination in Pine City. With over 8+ stores, 2 eateries and a host of exciting entertainment, visitors are spoilt for choice! At Pine City Mall you will find local South African retail, popular international brands. Pine City Mall also features an 15 cinema Ster Kinekor complex.
